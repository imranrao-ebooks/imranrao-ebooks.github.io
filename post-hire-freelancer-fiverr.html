How to Hire a Freelancer on Fiverr

June 6, 2026

Hiring the right freelancer on Fiverr saves time and money. The wrong freelancer costs both. This guide helps you choose wisely.

Check the Seller's Profile

Look at the profile picture and description. Real sellers share clear photos and write specific bios. Avoid generic profiles with no details.

Read Reviews Carefully

Do not just look at star ratings. Read negative reviews. One or two bad reviews are normal. A pattern of the same complaint is a red flag.

Ask for a Sample

Before placing a large order, request a small sample. Good freelancers will share examples of their work. Scammers or low-quality sellers will make excuses.

Start with a Small Test Order

Never start with your full project. Order a small task first. If the quality is good, then order the rest. This protects your budget.

Stay on Fiverr

Never communicate or pay outside Fiverr. Fiverr's buyer protection only works when you stay on the platform. Scammers will try to move you off-site.

Follow these five steps and you will hire better freelancers every time.
